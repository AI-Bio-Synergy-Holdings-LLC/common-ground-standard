Responsible AI Infrastructure

Tests whether energy, water, land, grid capacity, data practices, labor conditions, and community effects are visible before an infrastructure claim is treated as responsible.

Start with: Which impact should be measured before a pilot begins, and what result should make it pause?

Public-review compact

Campus AI Governance

Tests whether campus AI preserves authorship and learning, protects student data, limits surveillance, respects workers, explains consequential uses, and gives affected people a real governance role.

Start with: Which campus AI use most needs a clearer right, rule, notice, or appeal path?

03

Public-review clause

AI Benefit Sharing

Tests whether claimed gains are measured against a credible baseline, preserve rights and service quality, give affected people decision authority, route a defined benefit, and leave a public receipt.

Start with: What evidence and decision right would make a benefit claim genuinely reviewable?

05

Public-review checklist

Anti-Capture Risks

Tests whether an interested actor could control governance, data, evidence, benefits, reviewers, or public claims and receive the appearance of legitimacy without enforceable safeguards.

Start with: Which safeguard is easiest to weaken, and what would make it enforceable?
