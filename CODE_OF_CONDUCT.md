# Code of Conduct

Status: Public-review community standard
Date: June 23, 2026

## Purpose

The Common Ground Standard repository exists for public-interest critique, standards hardening, and careful review of AI infrastructure, campus AI governance, benefit-sharing, evidence, and anti-capture safeguards.

Participants are expected to help make the review process rigorous, specific, and usable without making the repository hostile, exclusionary, or unsafe for affected communities and reviewers.

## Expected Behavior

Participants should:

- be direct, specific, and evidence-minded;
- critique ideas, claims, clauses, and risks rather than attacking people;
- identify uncertainty and assumptions clearly;
- respect student, worker, community, tribal, ecological, and personal-data boundaries;
- avoid endorsement, certification, partnership, or affiliation claims unless explicitly authorized;
- help keep discussions focused on the public-review draft and its safeguards.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment, threats, intimidation, or personal attacks;
- discriminatory, demeaning, or exclusionary language;
- disclosure of private, confidential, personal, protected, or restricted data;
- submitting trade secrets or materials the contributor does not have permission to share;
- using review participation to imply endorsement, certification, partnership, or official status;
- coordinated attempts to dilute safeguards, suppress critique, or capture the review process.

## Scope

This code applies to repository issues, pull requests, discussions if enabled later, public review materials, and project-related communication channels controlled by the repository maintainers.

## Reporting

Do not report private safety, conduct, or security concerns through public issues.

Send private reports to:

```text
security@aibiosynergy.com
```

Use the subject line `Common Ground Standard conduct report` and include the relevant links, context, and any requested confidentiality constraints.

## Enforcement

AI-Bio Synergy Holdings LLC, as temporary repository steward during public review, may moderate, hide, close, or remove contributions that violate this code or the repository's public-review boundaries.

Possible actions include:

- requesting clarification or edits;
- closing or locking an issue or pull request;
- removing sensitive information;
- limiting participation;
- escalating security or legal concerns outside the public repository.

Enforcement should preserve legitimate critique, including strong criticism of the draft, while protecting the integrity and safety of the review process.
