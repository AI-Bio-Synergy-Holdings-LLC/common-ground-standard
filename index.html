The Standard Is An Evidence Loop

Common Ground Standard is organized around a practical test: public-interest AI claims should be bounded by governance, proof, benefit routing, and auditability.

01

Infrastructure Safeguards

Ask what an AI-enabling project consumes, exposes, and owes to affected places.

02

Campus Governance

Define student, worker, faculty, and institutional rights before deployment pressure hardens.

03

Benefit Sharing

Route productivity, data, infrastructure, or economic gains into visible public benefit.

04

Assurance And Audit

Turn claims into reviewable receipts, no-go conditions, and correction paths.
