Review Guide

The most valuable review is specific, critical, and constructive. The question is not whether the draft sounds good. The question is whether it can prevent weak claims, capture, and accountability gaps.

Read the review guide
  • Test whether each safeguard is specific enough to enforce.
  • Flag where data, student, worker, community, or ecological rights are weak.
  • Identify what evidence should be required before alignment claims.
  • Propose no-go conditions that should pause, redesign, or reject a use case.
