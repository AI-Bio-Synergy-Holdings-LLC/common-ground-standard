# Funding And Stewardship Readiness

Status: Public boundary document
Date: June 21, 2026

## Current Position

The Common Ground Standard is a public-review draft incubated by AI-Bio Synergy Holdings LLC.

AI-Bio Synergy Holdings LLC may act as founder steward, IP steward, domain administrator, repository administrator, and temporary project incubator during the public-review phase.

This incubation role does not mean that AI-Bio Synergy Holdings LLC is the permanent public governance body, neutral standards authority, certification authority, fiscal sponsor, or recipient of public-interest funding for the long-term Common Ground Standard effort.

## Why This Boundary Matters

The project needs founder control while its rights, marks, claims, and draft materials are still being protected.

The project also needs a credible pathway toward independent review, anti-capture safeguards, and a public-interest governance structure before foundations, agencies, universities, civil society organizations, infrastructure actors, or AI companies are asked to rely on it.

## AI-Bio Stewardship During Public Review

During the public-review phase, AI-Bio Synergy Holdings LLC may hold or administer:

- founder-controlled IP and background materials;
- Common Ground Standard domain and repository administration;
- public draft publication;
- trademark and certification-mark planning;
- private/internal working materials;
- legal, technical, and review-readiness coordination;
- initial operating costs.

## What Is Not Claimed

This public-review posture does not claim that AI-Bio Synergy Holdings LLC is:

- an independent standards body;
- a nonprofit fiscal sponsor;
- a tax-exempt charitable recipient;
- a certification authority;
- a neutral public governance board;
- a representative body for affected communities, students, workers, tribes, agencies, universities, foundations, or industry actors.

## Target Structure

The preferred long-term path is staged:

1. Founder/IP stewardship and public-review publication remain controlled during early review.
2. Legitimacy reviewers stress-test the draft before industry or major funding outreach.
3. A suitable independent fiscal and governance pathway is evaluated.
4. Public funding, if activated, is separated from standards control and certification claims.
5. Certification or conformance use, if developed later, requires formal criteria, evidence, governance records, and auditability.

## Open Collective And Sponsor Button Status

The repository should not add an active GitHub Sponsor button or Open Collective funding link until the funding boundary is ready.

This means the repository intentionally does not include `.github/FUNDING.yml` at this stage.

An Open Collective or similar public funding channel should be activated only after:

- a fiscal host or legal-accounting pathway is selected;
- permitted uses of funds are published;
- donor influence limits are published;
- conflict-of-interest rules are published;
- expense approval and disclosure practices are defined;
- the project can clearly state that funding does not buy governance control, endorsement, certification, private access, or preferential influence.

## Future Funding Guardrails

If public funding is accepted later, funds should support public-review infrastructure and standards-hardening work such as:

- documentation;
- accessibility;
- public review operations;
- reviewer support;
- issue triage;
- legal and administrative review;
- public-interest research support;
- tooling that improves transparency and auditability.

Funding should not grant:

- endorsement;
- partnership status;
- certification rights;
- control over draft content;
- access to confidential materials;
- access to restricted data;
- influence over reviewer selection;
- priority treatment in pilots or conformance decisions.

## Safe Public Language

Acceptable public language:

> Common Ground Standard is a public-review draft incubated by AI-Bio Synergy Holdings LLC while independent governance and fiscal pathways are evaluated.

Avoid public language that says or implies:

- AI-Bio Synergy Holdings LLC is the permanent standards body;
- the project is already independently governed;
- the project is already fundable as a tax-deductible charitable effort;
- sponsors or donors are partners, reviewers, endorsers, or certified participants;
- funding creates influence over the standard.

## Review Trigger

This document should be reviewed before:

- adding `.github/FUNDING.yml`;
- launching an Open Collective;
- accepting public sponsorship;
- approaching foundations for funding;
- announcing fiscal sponsorship;
- announcing a consortium governance model;
- announcing certification or conformance marks.
