# Reviewer Questions

Status: Public-review draft

## General Questions

1. What claim in this packet feels strongest?
2. What claim feels too broad, vague, or premature?
3. What language sounds like public benefit but could enable capture?
4. What minimum evidence should be required before any actor can claim alignment?
5. What should be explicitly prohibited?
6. What would make this credible to skeptical students, workers, communities, and environmental reviewers?
7. What would make you worry this could become reputation cover for AI or infrastructure actors?

4. What data should be public, controlled, confidential, or restricted?
5. What would count as a real community benefit agreement?
6. What evidence should a public dashboard include?
7. What should certification require later?
