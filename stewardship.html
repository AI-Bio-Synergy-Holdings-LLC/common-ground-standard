Current Position

AI-Bio Synergy Holdings LLC may act as founder steward, IP steward, domain administrator, repository administrator, and temporary project incubator during the public-review phase. That role does not make AI-Bio Synergy Holdings LLC the permanent standards body, certification authority, nonprofit fiscal sponsor, or neutral public governance board.

Why this boundary matters

The project needs founder control while rights, marks, claims, and draft materials are protected. It also needs a credible path toward independent review and anti-capture safeguards before major funding, foundation, agency, university, or industry outreach.

AI-Bio Synergy Holdings LLC may steward now
  • Founder-controlled IP and background materials
  • Domain and repository administration
  • Public draft publication
  • Trademark and certification-mark planning
  • Private working materials and review-readiness coordination
Not claimed now

The public-review phase does not claim independent standards-body status, charitable tax status, certification authority, or representation of affected parties.

Target structure

Keep founder stewardship during review, evaluate independent fiscal and governance pathways, and separate funding from standards control and certification claims.
